Áiteanna Beannaithe

Man dear, there was some quare nice ragweeds about Newholland. Many' the summer's night when we were childer, we'd be up dancin' roun' them, an' the bats hoppin' aff us. ... Molloy'd be there o' coourse, the last o' the Church Street Lilters. He usedt'll fally us over the bridge. We knowed it was him cause we'd hear him practicing.

Caint ghalánta a chualathas taobh amuigh de Theach an Mhargaidh, i ndiaidh an mhisin. Nach trua ghéar gan oiread is nóta den phortaireacht béil a bheith le cluinstin thar leath-dhoirse Shráid na hEaglaise san lá atá inniu ann. D'imigh an ceol agus d'fhan an aitneach agus na buachalláin bhuí.
